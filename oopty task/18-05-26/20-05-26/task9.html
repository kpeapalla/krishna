To travel from Charles de Gaulle (CDG) Airport to the Eiffel Tower, you can take the RER B train from the airport to central Paris and then change to either the RER C train or the Paris Metro to reach the Eiffel Tower area. This is the most economical option and usually takes about 55 to 70 minutes.
